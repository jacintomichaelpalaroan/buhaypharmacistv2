Our Purpose

BuhayPharmacist is a website dedicated to promote the Profession of Pharmacy in the Philippines by providing details about the profession. Its scope includes a brief introduction about pharmacy and its value, schools offering the course, career opportunities, and other related resources that are important and uplifting to the pharmacy profession.
